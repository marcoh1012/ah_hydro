Audi A6 Miami Blue

Full vehicle wrap transformation on a sophisticated Audi A6, utilizing premium Miami Blue vinyl film from Vinyl Frog. The Vinyl Frog Miami Blue vinyl was meticulously applied, resulting in a complete color change from the original white to a stunning, head-turning Miami Blue finish. To create a striking contrast, the hood and roof were wrapped in a high-gloss black vinyl, further enhancing the Audi's sporty aesthetic.
